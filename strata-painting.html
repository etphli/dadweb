Strata Painting Specialists

Complete painting solutions for strata complexes and residential buildings. Our team specializes in managing large-scale projects while minimizing disruption to residents.

Strata Painting Experts You Can Trust

Strata painting requires specialized knowledge of body corporate regulations, resident coordination, and complex project management. Our team has successfully completed over 200 strata projects, from small townhouse complexes to large apartment buildings with hundreds of units. We understand the unique challenges of working in occupied buildings and have developed systems to minimize disruption while maintaining the highest quality standards.

We're fully licensed and insured for strata work, with comprehensive public liability coverage and all necessary certifications. Our project managers work closely with strata committees and building managers to ensure clear communication, detailed progress reporting, and full compliance with all regulations. We provide detailed quotes, progress photos, and completion certificates for body corporate records.
